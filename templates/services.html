Frequently Asked Questions

Is the content AI-written or human-written?

It's AI-assisted and human-edited. We use AI tools to draft at speed, then a human editor reviews, improves, and adds expertise before delivery. The result is indistinguishable from fully human-written content — and often better, because we can iterate faster.

Will the content rank on Google?

We can't guarantee rankings (no one can), but every article is keyword-targeted, properly structured, and includes on-page SEO elements. Our clients typically see first rankings within 6–10 weeks of publishing.

Do you offer white-label for agencies?

Yes. We work with agencies on a white-label basis. You receive articles with no AI Tools Empire branding. Wholesale rates available for orders of 10+ articles/month.

How do I provide topics and keywords?

You can provide your own keywords and topics, or let us handle research entirely. We'll build a content calendar for your niche and get your approval before writing starts.

What if I'm not happy with an article?

Unlimited revisions. We keep editing until you're satisfied. In practice, most articles need 0–1 rounds of revisions.

Can I cancel anytime?

Yes. Month-to-month billing, no contracts. Cancel before your next billing date and you won't be charged again.
