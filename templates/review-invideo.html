What Is InVideo AI?

InVideo AI is a browser-based video creation platform that uses AI to generate full videos from a text prompt. You describe what you want — topic, style, target platform — and it writes a script, selects stock footage, adds voiceover, and assembles the timeline automatically.

It launched in 2019 and has since pivoted heavily toward the AI-first workflow. As of 2026, it's one of the few tools where a complete beginner can go from idea to published YouTube video in under 15 minutes.

Who It's Best For

  • Faceless YouTube channel creators (listicles, explainers, news)
  • Social media managers handling multiple brand accounts
  • Affiliate marketers creating product review videos
  • Agencies that need high-volume, templated video output
  • Bloggers repurposing articles into video content

It's not ideal for cinematic projects, talking-head interviews, or anything requiring precision timeline editing.
