## 4. Pricing Strategy

### Phase 1: Review Acquisition (Week 1–2)

The goal is 10 reviews, not profit. Price aggressively to win your first orders:

| Gig | Launch Price | Target Orders |
|-----|-------------|---------------|
| AI Promo Video | Basic: $10 | 3 reviews |
| AI Voiceover | Basic: $7 | 3 reviews |
| SEO Blog Post | Basic: $15 | 2 reviews |
| Ad Copy Pack | Basic: $10 | 2 reviews |

- Offer 1 freebie per gig to get your first testimonial (limit to 1 per gig)
- Enable "Seller Plus" analytics once you hit 5 orders to see click-through rates

### Phase 2: Price Normalization (Week 3–4)

After 10 reviews with a 5-star average:

| Gig | Increase To | Reasoning |
|-----|-------------|-----------|
| AI Promo Video | Basic: $15–20 | Proven quality |
| AI Voiceover | Basic: $12–15 | Fast turnaround justifies it |
| SEO Blog Post | Basic: $25 | SEO value is high |
| Ad Copy Pack | Basic: $20 | Conversions = high perceived value |
| Social Media Pack | Basic: $25 | Recurring need = repeat buyers |

### Phase 3: Scale (Month 2+)

- Raise Premium packages to $75–$150
- Offer **monthly retainer packages** (e.g., "4 blog posts/month for $180")
- Upsell buyers on the social media package after delivering ad copy
- Use **Fiverr Pro** application once you have 50+ reviews at 4.8+ stars
- Create **bundle gigs**: e.g., "Blog post + social captions + promo video = $120"
